## Banking System with Loan Management
- **Problem Statement:** Extend a bank system to manage loans. Track different loan types, repayment schedules, and interest calculations. Integrate with existing account management.
- **OOP Concepts Tested:** Inheritance, Polymorphism, Composition, Abstraction
- **Optional Requirements:** Differentiate between personal, home, and vehicle loans; handle interest calculations dynamically; maintain relationship between accounts and loans.
